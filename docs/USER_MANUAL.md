# User Manual

## Sign in

1. Open the application URL.
2. Enter email and password.
3. If 2FA is enabled, enter the 6-digit authenticator code.

Demo: `admin@demo.local` / `Admin@123`

## Dashboard

View KPIs: sales today/week/month, inventory value, low stock, charts, top products and customers.

## Point of Sale

1. Open **POS** from the sidebar.
2. Click **Open Shift** at the start of the day.
3. Scan barcodes (focus with **F2**) or tap products.
4. Adjust quantities, apply discount, choose payment method.
5. Press **Charge** or **F9** to complete.
6. If offline, sales queue locally and can be synced later.

## Products

- Search by name, SKU, or barcode.
- Add products with cost/selling price and type (Product, Service, Drug).
- Monitor stock columns and status.

## Inventory

- Review **low stock** against reorder levels.
- Review **expiring batches** (pharmacy/medical).

## Customers & Suppliers

Maintain CRM contacts for credit sales and procurement.

## Hospital

Register patients, view patient numbers, demographics, and blood group. Further clinical flows (appointments, prescriptions) use the same module APIs.

## Pharmacy

Lists drug-type products, Rx flags, and stock for dispensing workflows.

## Laboratory

View lab orders and statuses.

## Accounting

Browse chart of accounts codes and balances.

## Reports

Select a report card and generate (PDF/Excel/CSV/Print hooks for extension).

## Settings

View company profile, branches, warehouses, and security overview. Toggle dark mode from the top bar.

## Sign out

Avatar menu → Logout.
